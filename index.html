BhashaPul Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your privacy

Last Updated: June 06, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BhashaPul ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our translation application.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the BhashaPul mobile application (the "App"). By using our App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide

  • Text for Translation: The text you input for translation.
  • Voice Recordings: When you use voice input features, we temporarily process your voice recordings to convert speech to text.
  • Language Preferences: Your selected source and target languages.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Translation History: Records of your translations stored locally on your device.
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the app, such as which features you use most frequently.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device (model, OS version) to optimize app performance.

Note: All translation history and favorites are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a copy of your translation history on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Translation Services: Processing your text and speech inputs to deliver translation results.
  • Improving Our Services: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience.
  • Technical Support: Addressing technical issues and providing customer support.
  • Service Personalization: Remembering your preferences and tailoring the service to your needs.

Third-Party Services

BhashaPul uses certain third-party services to provide translation capabilities:

Translation Services

We may use third-party translation APIs to process your translation requests. When utilizing these services, the text you submit for translation is transmitted to these providers.

Text-to-Speech and Speech Recognition

For text-to-speech and speech recognition functionality, we may use:

  • Your device's built-in text-to-speech engines
  • Google's Speech Recognition services
  • VoiceRSS API (as a fallback text-to-speech service)

When you use speech features, audio data may be processed by these services to convert between speech and text.

Important: When network text-to-speech services are used as a fallback, the text to be spoken is transmitted to these services. We only use these services when device-based TTS is not available or does not support your selected language.

Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns. These analytics are anonymized and do not contain your personal data or the content of your translations.

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security of your information:

  • Local Storage: Your translation history and favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Temporary Processing: Voice recordings are temporarily processed and are not permanently stored.
  • Transmission Security: When data is transmitted to translation services, we use industry-standard encryption protocols.

While we implement safeguards to protect your information, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Manage Translation History: You can view, delete individual entries, or clear your entire translation history from within the app.
  • Voice Input: Voice input features are optional and can be disabled.
  • App Permissions: You can modify app permissions (like microphone access) through your device settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccuracies
  • The right to deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

Children's Privacy

BhashaPul is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information about your child, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

If we make material changes to this policy, we will provide notice through the App or by other means. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this Privacy Policy was last revised.
