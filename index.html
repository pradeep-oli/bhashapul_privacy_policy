BhashaPul Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

BhashaPul respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. We do not collect any personal information or data from you when you use our app. The app only requires microphone access for voice input translation and camera access for text recognition from images, but these data are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted or stored by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. Your translations and voice inputs are processed locally and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Storage

Your translation history and favorites are stored locally on your device only. You can delete these at any time through the app interface. We do not have access to this data, nor is it backed up on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses LibreTranslate and other open-source translation services to provide translation functionality. When you translate text, the text may be sent to these services' servers for translation. We use only reputable services and do not share your data with any other third parties. All translation services are used solely for the purpose of providing translation functionality.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the 'Last Updated' date.
